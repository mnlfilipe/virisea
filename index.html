What We Deliver

End-to-end pool
management.

01
Pool Formation & Counterparty Matching
We assess your GHG intensity profile and source matching counterparties — within your group or from our network — to form the most cost-efficient pool.
02
Commercial Structuring
We negotiate the pooling agreement — surplus valuation, cost allocation, and commercial terms that are fair, transparent, and bankable.
03
Certificate Management & Reporting
Full lifecycle management of FuelEU certificates — transfers, surrender obligations, and audit-ready compliance documentation.
04
Multi-Year Pool Strategy
As GHG targets tighten through 2030 and 2050, we model the trajectory and keep your compliance position structurally sound year after year.
